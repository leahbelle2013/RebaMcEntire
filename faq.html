When is Reba's birthday?
Top
Reba was born March 28th, 1955. She was born in McAlester, Oklahoma and was raised on a ranch in Chockie, Oklahoma. Reba is now 68 years old.
What did Reba want to be when she grew up?
Top
Reba wanted to be a teacher when she was a child. Once finishing high school, Reba enrolled in college at Southeastern Oklahoma State University. She majored in elementary education with a minor in music. She completed student teaching and later graduated with a Bachelor's degree.
How old was Reba when she started singing?
Top
Young Reba started performing at school, beginning in first grade when she sang "Away in a Manger" at an elementary school Christmas pageant. In fifth grade, she joined the 4-H club and won first place in the Junior Act Division for singing "My Sweet Little Alice Blue Gown".
Does Reba have any siblings?
Top
Reba was the third of four children born to Clark and Jacqueline McEntire.
Is Reba currently married?
Top
Reba is currently divorced. She was previously married to Charlie Battles from 1976 to 1987 and music manager Narvel Blackstock from 1989 to 2015.
Does Reba have any children?
Top
Reba's only biological child, Shelby, was born on February 23, 1990 in Nashville, Tennessee. She also has 5 stepchildren from her previous marriage including Coty, Lance, Shawna, Brandon, and Chassidy.
How many records has Reba sold?
Top
Widely recognized as the "Queen of Country" by various media outlets, she has sold an estimated 90 million records worldwide throughout her career, making her the 2nd best selling female country artist in history.
How many number one songs does Reba have?
Top
In her 45-year career, McEntire has garnered 24 number one singles on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart, the second most number one hits by a female artist behind Dolly Parton with 25. In addition, McEntire holds the record for the most top 10 hits by a female country artist, surpassing Parton's record.
